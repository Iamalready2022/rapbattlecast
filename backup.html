Our Story

Founded in 2020 by a collective of battle rap veterans and tech innovators, RapBattleCast emerged from a vision to revolutionize the battle rap scene. What started as a small online community during the global lockdown has evolved into the largest digital battle rap platform, hosting over 1,000 events and launching countless careers.

Our mission is to elevate battle rap culture through innovation, authenticity, and community. We believe in preserving the raw essence of battle rap while providing modern tools and opportunities for artists to showcase their talent. Our core values of respect, creativity, and excellence drive everything we do, from our judging system to our community guidelines.
